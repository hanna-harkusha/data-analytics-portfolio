# Revenue Metrics Dashboard (Tableau) 



### Objective: 



To analyze gaming product revenue and user payment behavior through an interactive Tableau dashboard.



The goal was to monitor key monetization metrics, track monthly revenue dynamics, identify churn patterns, and evaluate how different user groups contributed to revenue changes.



### Tools & Methods: 



- **Tableau Public** – dashboard creation and data visualization

- **Calculated Fields** – custom metrics for revenue, churn, paid users, ARPPU, and MRR movement

- **Filters** – interactive filtering by month, language, and age

- **Dual-Axis Charts** – combined visualization of revenue and paid users

- **Bar Charts & Line Charts** – visualization of monthly trends and metric comparisons

- **Dashboard Layout** – structured overview of key product revenue metrics



### Data Description: 



- **Source:** Educational gaming payment dataset

- **Main Tables:**

  - `games_paid_users` – user-level information

  - `games_payments` – payment transactions and revenue data

- **Main Fields Used:**

  - User ID

  - Game name

  - Payment month

  - Revenue

  - Previous payment month

  - Previous revenue

  - Age

  - Language



### Dashboard Metrics



The dashboard includes the following metrics:



- Monthly Recurring Revenue

- Average Revenue Per Paid User

- New Paid Users

- New MRR

- Churned Users

- Churned Revenue

- Expansion MRR

- Contraction MRR

- Back from Churn MRR



### Results



- Built an interactive dashboard for monitoring product revenue performance

- Visualized monthly revenue and paid user dynamics

- Created calculated fields for key monetization and churn metrics

- Added filters for age, language, and month to support deeper analysis

- Compared expansion and contraction revenue to understand positive and negative MRR movement

- Highlighted churn and returning user patterns for further product analysis
