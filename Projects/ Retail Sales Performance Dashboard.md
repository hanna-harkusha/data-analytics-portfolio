# Retail Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI) 📊



### Objective:



To build an interactive Power BI report for analyzing retail sales performance, budget execution, product structure, inventory, and regional revenue dynamics.



### Tools & Methods:



**Power BI, Power Query, DAX, data modeling, calendar table, product and date hierarchies, slicers, conditional formatting, navigation buttons, tooltips, drillthrough, map visualization.**



### Data Description:



The report is based on a sample retail dataset with sales, budget, inventory, products, managers, departments, warehouses, and calendar tables.



### Report Overview:



The report includes analysis of actual revenue, budget revenue, plan vs actual deviation, previous year revenue, deviation from previous year, product hierarchy share, inventory balances, cumulative sales, rolling-period revenue, and revenue by city, region, and product type.



### Dashboard Pages:



**Plan vs Actual, Dynamics Analysis, Recent Sales Analysis, City / Region Revenue, Product Sales Structure, Tooltip Page, Title Page with navigation buttons.**



### Results:



Built a complete Power BI report with a structured data model, cleaned and transformed data in Power Query, created DAX measures for sales, budget, previous year, inventory and rolling-period analysis, added interactive filters, map visualization, KPI cards, matrices, charts, conditional formatting, tooltips and drillthrough.
